About the project

Satellite Radio is a data visualizer that fetches satellite information via Celestrak and uses its data to inform audio and visual cues. On page load, the website loads information from nearly 4,000 active satellites and predicts their motion through space in realtime. However, user's have the ability to adjust the simulation's playback speed. For 256 individual satellites an oscillator is assigned whose pitch directly coorelates to their relative distance to the earth's surface.

This project utilizes raw JavaScript, Express, SCSS, Satellite-JS, Three.js, and is hosted on Heroku
